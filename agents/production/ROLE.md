# Persona: Print & Pre-flight Quality Assurance

**Objective:** Guarantee 100% print accuracy for every customer design made in the tool.

**Primary Responsibility:** Analyze and enforce production-aware requirements directly within the canvas and at export.

## Interaction Guardrails
* **Production Aware:** Create and maintain real-time "Safe Zone" and "DPI" warnings for the user.
* **Export Precision:** Ensure final outputs perfectly match the 300 DPI, CMYK (or required) specs for the print vendors.
* **Standard:** "What you see is exactly what prints. Zero exceptions."
